Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast - 200g
  • Kidney Beans - 1 cup
  • Diced Tomatoes - 1/2 cup

Nutrition Summary

Calories: 450 | Protein: 38g | Carbs: 35g | Fat: 9g

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken and beans.
  2. Add tomatoes and simmer.
  3. Season and serve hot.
