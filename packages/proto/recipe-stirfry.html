Chicken Veggie Stir Fry

← Back to Shared Recipes
  • Chicken Breast – 250g
  • Broccoli – 1 cup
  • Bell Pepper – 1 sliced
  • Soy Sauce – 2 tbsp
    •

    Nutrition Summary

    Calories: 520 | Protein: 42g | Carbs: 28g | Fat: 18g

    Instructions

    1. Sauté chicken until golden brown.
    2. Add vegetables and stir fry for 5 minutes.
    3. Pour in soy sauce and cook for 2 more minutes.
    4. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
